## 1. Product Definition

- **Product:** TaxClaim Pro — Automated Form 843 Generator & Penalty Abatement Platform
- **Domain:** taxclaim.virtuallaunch.pro
- **Core promise:** Tax professionals get a hosted landing page that auto-generates IRS Form 843 refund claims for their taxpayers — penalty abatement on autopilot
- **Revenue model:** SaaS subscription (monthly)

## 2. Target Audience (ICP)

**Primary:** Tax professionals (CPAs, EAs, tax preparers) who handle individual taxpayer penalty abatement
- Currently doing Form 843 manually or not at all
- Want to offer penalty relief as an additional revenue stream
- Need year-round revenue beyond tax season

**Secondary:** Tax resolution firms and enrolled agents specializing in IRS compliance

## 3. Problems Solved

| Pain Point | TaxClaim Pro Solution |
|------------|----------------------|
| Manual Form 843 preparation is tedious | Auto-generated PDFs from taxpayer data |
| Penalty calculations require IRS transcript review | Automated penalty & interest calculations |
| No client-facing intake process | Branded hosted landing page per professional |
| Revenue gaps between tax seasons | Year-round penalty abatement service |
| No dashboard for taxpayer tracking | Taxpayer dashboard with status updates |
