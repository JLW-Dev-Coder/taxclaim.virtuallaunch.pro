How Your Clients Claim Their Refund

Follow these steps to generate, print, and mail your IRS Form 843 safely and correctly.

1

Pull Your Transcripts

  • Access your IRS transcript at irs.gov/get-transcript or call 800-908-9946
  • Print your Account Transcript for tax years 2020 through 2023
2

Confirm Your Eligibility

  • Review penalties or interest assessed between Jan 20, 2020 – July 10, 2023
  • Look for Failure-to-File, Failure-to-Pay, or interest charges
  • If penalties or interest exist, click the payment link to access the Form 843 generator
3

Generate Sample Form 843

  • If penalties or interest exist, continue to Step 3 below
  • All fields pre-populated referencing the case ruling
  • Built with exact mapping fields for you or us to complete the official form
4

Print & Mail

  • Download and print your completed Form 843
  • Mailing address auto-calculated based on your state
  • Mail the form before July 10, 2026
